M3A1 Armored Scout Vehicle, this is one of the symbols of allied forces during the WW2.

This package contains 2 models, with tent roof and without. 
Vehicle comes with interior.
Wheels and doors are detached.
Comes with a scene and prefabs for both models. 
Poly count is 6070 triangles.

Textures include Diffuse, Normal and Specular maps.
